Girls Science Interactive

Girls Science Interactive is an organization that provides free STEM summer camps for elementary and middle school girls to spark their interest in science. So far, we have hosted 20 free STEM summer camps and after school programs in Florida, Kentucky, Louisiana, New Jersey, New York, and Pennsylvania.

You can visit our website here. And check out our Facebook page.

Femmehacks 2017

What is Femmehacks?

FemmeHacks is a beginner-friendly, student hackathon for female-identifying and non-binary individuals in the Philly/NY/NJ area, hosted by the University of Pennsylvania's Women in Computer Science. We had the opportunity to attend and expand our knowledge in coding and html.
